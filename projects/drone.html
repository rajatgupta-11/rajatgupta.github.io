Project Parameters

    • Payload Delivery Accuracy – Circular Error Probable (CEP) ≤ 15 cm at ≤ 3 m altitude
    • Autonomous Navigation – Fully autonomous waypoint following using onboard flight controller and GPS
    • Vision-Guided Target Lock – Real-time object detection and tracking using onboard camera + OpenCV
    • Payload Release Mechanism – Electrically actuated, lightweight, and tested for ≥ 100 release cycles without failure
    • Failsafe Systems – Automatic Return-to-Home (RTH) or hover/land in event of signal loss or critical error
    • Flight Time – Minimum 5 minutes continuous operation with payload under standard conditions
    • Payload Capacity – At least 250 g net payload weight
    • Durability & Field Readiness – Able to withstand moderate wind (up to 20 km/h) and light rain for at least one mission
